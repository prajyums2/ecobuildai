## 1. EARTHWORK & FOUNDATION

### 1.1 Excavation
```
Footing Size: 1.2m × 1.2m × 0.3m (raft footing)
Number of Footings: 4 (columns) + 1 (wall footing)
Footing Volume: 4 × (1.2 × 1.2 × 0.3) + (20 × 0.6 × 0.3)
              = 4 × 0.432 + 3.6
              = 5.328 cum

Excavation Depth: 1.2m below GL
Excavation Volume: 5.328 × 1.2 × 1.5 (sides) = 9.59 cum
```

### 1.2 PCC Bed
```
PCC Below Footing: 100mm thick, M15
PCC Area: 5.328 / 0.3 = 17.76 sqm
PCC Volume: 17.76 × 0.1 = 1.78 cum
```

### 1.3 Backfilling
```
Backfill Volume: 9.59 - 5.328 - 1.78 = 2.48 cum
```
